# TacXO Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 29, 2026

This policy describes how **TacXO** (“the app”) handles information. TacXO is developed by milky-way-66.

## Summary

TacXO is fully offline. We do **not** collect, store, or share your personal data. We do **not** use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

## Information the app stores on your device

The app saves your game preferences locally using iOS **UserDefaults**, including:

- Board size and win length
- Game mode (2 Players or vs Neighbor)
- Language preference

This data stays on your device. It is not transmitted to us or any third party. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

## Information we do not collect

TacXO does not:

- Require an account or sign-in
- Access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera
- Send data over the internet
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- Share data with advertisers or data brokers

## Children

TacXO does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Third-party content

The app includes licensed sound effects (see [ATTRIBUTIONS.md](TacXO/Resources/ATTRIBUTIONS.md)). Those assets are bundled in the app and are not loaded from external servers at runtime.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
